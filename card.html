Royal Bengal Tiger

Tigers have a better memory than any other animal including humans.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Tigris Tigris
  • Average Length: 270 to 310 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands to Forests

Bengal tigers are distinct with orange, white, and black stripes. They are some of the biggest tigers in the world and can weigh up to 325 kg.
